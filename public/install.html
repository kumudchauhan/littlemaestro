LittleMaestro

Music app for little ones

iPhone / iPad Safari

  1. Open Safari and go to the app link below
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen — tap it to open!
Tip: The app runs fullscreen — no browser bar, no accidental URL touches! Perfect for little hands.

Android Chrome

  1. Open Chrome and go to the app link below
  2. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top-right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap "Install" to confirm
  5. Find the app on your home screen and tap to play!

Desktop Chrome / Edge

  1. Open the app link in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon (small + icon) in the address bar
  3. Click "Install" to add it as a desktop app
